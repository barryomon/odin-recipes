Tomato Stew

This tomato stew is easy to make and tastes great! This is a hearty stew. Hope you like it! I use two beef tenderloin filets for the stew beef.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 6 cups beef broth
  • 1(11.5ounce)cubed beef stew meat

Steps

  1. tost beef with salt
  2. pepper and flour to coat
  3. heat oil in large pot
  4. cook coated beef in oil
  5. stirring constantly until brown on all sides
  6. pour in broth and tomato juice
  7. stir in mixed veg,potato and cabbage
  8. bring to boil
  9. reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour
  10. leave to cool down
